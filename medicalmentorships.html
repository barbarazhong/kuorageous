Mentorship

For medical students applying to residency programs

Contact us for pricing and package details

  • Live 1-hour "getting to know your profile" strategy session with advice on admissions process only: choice of residency programs to apply, geographical ideals, USMLEs, post-residency career choices, fellowships, etc.
  • Live 1-hour mock interview with verbal feedback (no audio recording) with single interviewer style
  • Review of work/activities on your application form
  • Review of personal statement for theme and concepts, and proper English (maximum: 2 drafts and must allow two-week turnaround period for each draft)
  • Maximum: Application to five programs
  • Unlimited general advice (by email) that is prompt and responsive
  • Always personalized, customized attention
  • Proper professional etiquette (interviews, asking recommenders for letters, addressing interviewers, etc.)
  • Coping with stress (residency application process)
  • Loan deferrals and forgiveness
  • Proper professional etiquette (interviews, asking recommenders for letters, addressing residency program directors, etc.)
  • Letters of recommendation (review)
  • Residency program list and program choice decisions
  • Loan deferrals and forgiveness
  • Post-interview advice
  • Post-admission advice or post-rejection scrambling
  • Access to my own growing network of medical students, residents in training, and attending physicians in medical institutions throughout the country. Depending on which institution, I might be able to connect you with a colleague there to answer your specific questions about that residency program!

  • Proper professional etiquette (interviews, asking recommenders for letters, addressing interviewers, etc.)
  • Coping with stress (job search, head hunters, fellowship application process, what private practice is like, etc.)
  • Letters of recommendation (review)
  • Fellowship program list and program choice decisions
  • Post-interview advice
  • Access to my own growing network of residents in training and attending physicians in medical institutions throughout the country. Depending on which institution, I might be able to connect you with a colleague there to answer your specific questions about that residency program!

1. There will be no refunds, partial or complete, once services have begun to be rendered. If you decide midway through the application process that you are not interested anymore after all, we can discuss whether it is feasible to apply the remainder of your unused services for applying to schools of another discipline
2. Unfortunately, we do not provide “a la carte” services where you can pick and choose to subscribe to a specific service, e.g. essay edits, mock interviews. We provide comprehensive packages because the best way to help you is to polish every aspect of your application. We want to maximize your chances of admissions. Being involved in only bits and pieces deters us from discovering potential weaknesses in your application. Your success is key to our success!
3. Much of this mentorship can be achieved remotely, if geographic location bars you from in-person meetings. So if you are not physically located in New York City but are still interested in enrolling my assistance, please contact us anyway, so we can discuss possible options.
