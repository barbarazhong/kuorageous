If you decide midway through the application process that you are not interested in applying to that discipline or specialty anymore, we can discuss whether it is feasible to apply the remainder of your unused services for applying to schools of another discipline.
  2. Although we now offer “a la carte” services where you can pick and choose to subscribe to a specific service, e.g. essay edits, mock interviews, we issue a clear disclaimer here. When we are permitted to be involved in only one aspect of your application process and do not no insight or access to the rest, we are limited in the way that we can help you to maximize your admissions potential. We provide comprehensive packages exactly because the best way to achieve success is to allow us orchestrate, synchronize, and polish every piece of your application. Being involved in only bits and pieces, such as is the case with a la carte services, deters us from discovering potential weaknesses in your application. Your success is our success!
  3. Much of this mentorship can be achieved remotely, if geographic location bars you from in-person meetings. So if you are not physically located in New York City but are still interested in enrolling my assistance, please contact us anyway, so we can discuss possible options.
