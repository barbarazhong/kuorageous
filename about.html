LCDR Karen Kuo, MD, Esq

Dr. Karen Kuo is a board-certified internist and wound care specialist with fifteen years of clinical experience. She actively educates and advises students who aspire to become physicians, physician assistants, dentists, and nurses. Their testimonials can be found at www.facebook.com/kuorageousMDs.


Her elite academic training has opened a world of career opportunities in medicine and beyond. Her additional experience of undergoing the law school admissions process augments her understanding of how graduate admissions process operates. She provides candid, firsthand insight into school (college, medical, law, graduate, undergraduate) admissions process, doctoring, American healthcare system, risk management, career options for physicians, legal profession, and more. Let us guide you in your higher pursuit!

Professional Experience

  • Lieutenant Commander, United States Public Health Services, Dept of Homeland Security
  • Clinical Director, Division of Immigration Health Services
  • Concierge medicine, private practice in Manhattan, NY
  • Outpatient internal medicine, private practice in New York City and Long Island
  • Expert witness for medical malpractice cases
  • Adjunct teaching faculty professor, York College, City University of New York

The Wall of Success: Role Models

Kuorageous’ proud network of successful elites adds real world insight and serves as invaluable resource for our students. The access to working professionals of varied industries affords unique opportunities and practical wisdom beyond that typically provided by the average advisor whose experience is rooted solely in the admissions process. Whatever your interest, we will attempt to find a real working professional in that industry willing to offer “big sister/brother” insight.

Dr. Asadi is a board-certified dermatologist who specializes in Mohs micrographic surgery for the treatment of skin cancer.
