Mentorships

Empower yourself with the assistance of a personal admissions coach to achieve your dreams.


It is never too early to start your application process!
If you are a freshman or sophomore in college, then this is the best time to start acquiring practical/internship/clinical experience and enrolling with research opportunities.
If you are a high school student interested in pursuing a professional career, then what better time than now to think about which college will pave the road to success and what extracurricular activities will lay the foundation? We can help you with your college applications, in the same vein as helping undergraduates with their graduate (e.g. medical) school applications!
While the mentorship packages provide substantial guidance in the technical aspects of the actual application, the key to success actually lies in the foundational groundwork, well before even opening the application form! That is, appropriate course selection, impressive clinical experience, interesting research work, etc. Our mentorship guidance will assist you with these intangible but indispensable parts of your resume.

It is never too late to pursue your dreams!
Whether you are a post-baccalaureate, a college senior planning a gap year, or someone on his/her second career, we understand firsthand the trials and tribulations of being an untraditional student. Dr. Kuo has personally witnessed the challenges and triumphs of those who pursued medicine as a second career and others who gained worldly experience for a few years prior to applications. Allow us to advise you and to potentially connect you with similarly minded people who have now achieved success!

If you are interested in having Dr. Kuo or our team members visit your school or school club to give a brief introduction about the admissions process or you are a parent who would like to better understand the admissions process and how our mentorship can make a difference, please contact us at KuorageousMDs@gmail.com or use the “contact us” message page. Thank you.

Disclaimer:
We do not guarantee admissions. Ultimately, the admissions process is based on an individual’s particular strengths. What we do provide is mentorship, guidance, and experience, to refine and to polish your application so that you put your best foot forward!
